# SBI Fixed Deposit (High Yield)

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
SBI High Yield Fixed Deposits (FD) offer guaranteed, low-risk returns on lump-sum savings. With interest rates higher than standard savings accounts, FDs allow customers to grow their surplus cash safely while selecting interest payout options (monthly, quarterly, or on maturity).

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: Minimum age of 18 years. Available to minors under guardian supervision, senior citizens (60+), and corporates.
- **Minimum Deposit**: ₹1,000. No upper limit.
- **Tenure Options**: Ranges from 7 days to 10 years.

## Rates and Senior Citizen Benefits
- **Standard Interest Rates**: 6.00% to 7.25% per annum, depending on tenure.
- **Senior Citizen Rates**: Additional premium of 0.50% to 0.75% per annum on all tenures, yielding up to 8.00% on special tenures.
- **Premature Withdrawal**: Allowed, subject to a penalty of 0.50% to 1.00% on the applicable interest rate. No penalty for withdrawals after 5 years on select schemes.
- **FD-backed Loan**: Customers can obtain a loan or overdraft of up to 90% of their FD value at a highly competitive interest rate.
