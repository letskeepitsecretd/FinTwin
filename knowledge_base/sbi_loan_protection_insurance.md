# SBI Loan Protection Insurance

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
SBI Loan Protection Insurance is a group term life insurance plan designed to protect the borrower's family and dependents from the burden of outstanding debt in the event of the borrower's untimely death, accidental disability, or critical illness. In such events, the insurance payout settles the outstanding loan balance directly with the bank.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: Minimum entry age of 18 years, maximum entry age of 65 years. Maximum age at policy maturity is 70 years.
- **Linked Product**: Must be purchased concurrently with a new SBI Home Loan, Personal Loan, or Auto Loan, or added to an existing active loan package.
- **Premium Payment**: Single premium option (can be funded by the bank as part of the loan principal) or regular annual premium options.

## Features
- **Sum Assured**: Matches the outstanding loan amount at any point in time (Reducing Cover Option) or remains constant (Level Cover Option).
- **Tax Benefits**: Tax benefits are available under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act on the premium paid.
- **Exclusions**: Suicide within the first 12 months of the policy, pre-existing health conditions not declared at the time of application, and self-inflicted injuries.
