# SBI Overdraft Facility

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
The SBI Overdraft Facility is a credit line linked to a customer's salary or savings account. It allows customers to withdraw funds beyond their account balance, up to a pre-set limit. Interest is charged only on the utilized amount and for the exact duration of utilization, making it an excellent product for managing temporary cash flow mismatches.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Target Customer**: Available to corporate salary account holders, fixed deposit holders, or property owners.
- **Minimum Monthly Net Salary**: ₹25,000 for salaried overdraft.
- **Age**: Minimum age of 21 years, maximum age of 60 years.
- **Overdraft Limit**: Up to 3 times the net monthly salary (maximum ₹2,00,000) or up to 90% of the value of the security/fixed deposit.

## Terms and Rates
- **Interest Rates**: Salaried overdraft ranges from 12.50% to 15.00% per annum (floating rate calculated daily). Overdraft against Fixed Deposits is typically charged at 1.00% above the FD interest rate.
- **Repayment Tenure**: Renewed annually. Salaried overdrafts are automatically cleared upon salary credits.
- **Processing Fees**: Nil for FD-backed overdrafts; flat ₹1,000 + GST for salary-linked overdrafts.
