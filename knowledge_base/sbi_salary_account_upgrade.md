# SBI Salary Account Upgrade

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
The SBI Salary Account Upgrade allows salaried individuals to transition their existing standard bank account into a premium salary package. This product offers a host of benefits, including zero-balance requirements, higher daily transaction limits, preferential loan rates, and zero processing fees on select credit cards.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Employment**: Must be a full-time employee of a registered public/private corporate, government, or institutional partner.
- **Minimum Income**: Upgrade packages depend on monthly net credits:
  - **Silver**: Monthly salary between ₹10,000 and ₹25,000.
  - **Gold**: Monthly salary between ₹25,001 and ₹50,000.
  - **Diamond**: Monthly salary between ₹50,001 and ₹1,00,000.
  - **Platinum**: Monthly salary above ₹1,00,000.
- **Age**: Minimum age of 18 years, maximum age of 60 years.

## Benefits
- Zero balance requirement (no minimum average balance charges).
- Free unlimited ATM transactions at SBI and other bank ATMs.
- Up to 25% discount on locker rental charges.
- Automated sweep facility to Fixed Deposits for savings above ₹25,000.
- Compounding interest paid quarterly on savings balance.
