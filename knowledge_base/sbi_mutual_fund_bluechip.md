# SBI Mutual Fund — Bluechip

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
SBI Bluechip Fund is an equity mutual fund that invests primarily in large-cap companies with a proven track record, solid financial health, and established market presence. The fund aims to provide active investors with long-term capital growth through a diversified stock portfolio, mitigating volatility compared to mid- or small-cap funds.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: Minimum age of 18 years. Legal guardians can invest on behalf of minors.
- **Minimum Investment**:
  - **Lump Sum**: Minimum initial investment of ₹5,000.
  - **SIP Option**: Minimum recurring monthly investment of ₹1,000.
- **Investment Horizon**: Recommended for individuals with a medium-to-high risk tolerance and an investment horizon of at least 5 years.

## Fund Details
- **Historic Annualized Returns**: Long-term average returns typically range between 11% and 14% (subject to market forces).
- **Exit Load**: 1.00% exit penalty if units are redeemed or switched out within 1 year from the date of allotment; zero exit load after 1 year.
- **Taxation**: Returns are taxed as Long-Term Capital Gains (LTCG) at 10% (plus surcharges) for gains exceeding ₹1.25 Lakh in a financial year, provided the holding period is over 12 months.
