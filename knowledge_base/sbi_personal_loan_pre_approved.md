# SBI Personal Loan (Pre-approved)

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
The SBI Personal Loan (Pre-approved) is a quick-disposal, unsecured lending option for pre-selected bank customers. Designed for immediate personal expenses (medical emergencies, education, travel, or home renovation), it features digital documentation, rapid processing, and zero physical paperwork.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Target Customer**: Only pre-selected, active SBI savings/current account holders.
- **Age**: Minimum age of 21 years, maximum age of 58 years at the time of loan maturity.
- **Debt Burden**: The customer's total existing EMI obligations (including this loan) must not exceed 40% of their net monthly income (Debt-to-Income / DTI ratio).
- **Credit Score**: Minimum credit score of 720 is preferred. No past EMI defaults or debt write-offs on file.

## Terms and Rates
- **Interest Rates**: Ranges from 11.25% to 14.50% per annum (floating rate linked to Repo rate).
- **Loan Tenure**: Minimum 12 months, maximum 60 months.
- **Processing Fees**: Up to 1% of the loan amount, capped at ₹5,000.
- **Prepayment Charges**: 2% penalty if prepaid within the first 12 months; zero prepay charges after 24 months.
