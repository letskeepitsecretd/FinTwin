# SBI Home Loan Balance Transfer

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
SBI Home Loan Balance Transfer allows existing home loan borrowers of other banks, financial institutions, or housing finance companies to transfer their outstanding loan balances to SBI. This product helps customers take advantage of SBI's lower interest rates, reduced EMIs, and excellent customer service.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: Minimum age of 18 years, maximum age of 70 years at the time of loan maturity.
- **Minimum Outstanding Loan**: ₹5,00,000.
- **Repayment Record**: Minimum of 12 EMI payments must have been completed on time with the existing lender. No defaults or restructuring on record.
- **Employment**: Must be a salaried employee or a self-employed professional with a stable income.

## Terms and Rates
- **Interest Rates**: Floating rates from 8.40% to 9.15% per annum, depending on CIBIL score and loan amount.
- **Tenure**: Up to 30 years (or up to retirement age for salaried individuals).
- **Processing Fees**: Flat processing fee of ₹10,000 + GST.
- **Prepayment Charges**: Zero prepayment charges on floating-rate home loans.
