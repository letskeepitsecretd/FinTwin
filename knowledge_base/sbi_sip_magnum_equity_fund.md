# SBI SIP — Magnum Equity Fund

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
SBI Systematic Investment Plan (SIP) in the Magnum Equity Fund is an open-ended equity fund that aims to provide long-term capital appreciation. By investing a fixed amount regularly (monthly or quarterly), customers benefit from rupee cost averaging and power of compounding in large-cap and select mid-cap Indian stocks.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: Minimum age of 18 years. Accounts can be opened in the name of minors by a legal guardian.
- **Minimum Investment**: Starting from ₹500 per month.
- **Investment Horizon**: Highly recommended for customers with an investment horizon of 3 years or longer.

## Investment Details
- **Typical Annualized Returns**: Long-term historic average rates range from 12% to 15% (subject to market risks; past performance does not guarantee future returns).
- **Exit Load**: 1.00% if redeemed or switched out within 1 year of allotment; Nil after 1 year.
- **Lock-in Period**: None (unlike ELSS tax-saving schemes, which have a 3-year lock-in).
- **Risk Category**: Very High (Equity exposure). Ideal for young professionals or mid-career individuals looking to accumulate long-term wealth.
