# SBI Credit Card (Signature)

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
The SBI Credit Card (Signature) is a premium lifestyle card designed for high-net-worth individuals and corporate employees. It offers accelerated reward points, complimentary airport lounge access, dining benefits, and comprehensive travel insurance cover.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Employment**: Salaried corporate professionals or self-employed business owners with a verified income source.
- **Minimum Monthly Income**: ₹1,00,000 net monthly credit.
- **Age**: Minimum age of 21 years, maximum age of 65 years.
- **Credit Score**: Minimum CIBIL score of 750 is required.

## Key Fees and Charges
- **Annual Membership Fee**: ₹4,999 + GST (reversed if annual spend exceeds ₹4,00,000).
- **Interest Charges (APR)**: 3.50% per month (42% per annum) on outstanding balances.
- **Cash Advance Limit**: Up to 30% of the credit limit.
- **Foreign Currency Markup**: 1.99% (among the lowest premium credit card markup rates in the market).
