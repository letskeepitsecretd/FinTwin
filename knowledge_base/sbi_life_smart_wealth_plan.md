# SBI Life Smart Wealth Plan

*NOTE: These are illustrative/synthetic product descriptions created for this project, not official SBI documents or rates.*

## Overview
SBI Life Smart Wealth Plan is an individual, non-participating, unit-linked life insurance product (ULIP). It combines the benefits of life insurance coverage with investment growth, allowing policyholders to allocate premiums across various fund choices (equity, debt, balanced) depending on their risk appetite.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: Minimum entry age of 7 years, maximum entry age of 60 years. Age at maturity must not exceed 70 years.
- **Minimum Premium**: Starting from ₹2,500 per month or ₹30,000 annually.
- **Lock-in Period**: Mandatory lock-in period of 5 years. No partial withdrawals or policy surrenders are permitted before the end of the 5th policy year.

## Key Features
- **Life Cover**: Sum Assured of 10 times the annualized premium is payable to the nominee in case of the policyholder's death.
- **Surrender Values**: Guaranteed surrender value is applicable only after the completion of the 5-year lock-in period.
- **Tax Exemptions**: Deductions on premiums paid under Section 80C, and tax-free maturity benefits under Section 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act (subject to premium limits).
- **Maturity Payout**: Fund Value is paid to the policyholder upon surviving the policy term.
